Random Life Pro

Life is random. You don't know who you will meet, which company you will worked in, what you will get in return.It is unknown, and this random makes wonderful fate.

RandomLifePro rules

1. Tap the screen, get the big ball, when the normal ball collided the big ball, you will get score.

2. Tap the screen for 1 seconds, there will show menu and pause the game.

3. Every score multiplies the round number, then add them up, you will get total score.

4. If you get all balls in one round game, you will get more scores.

5. Obtained score minus used score, you will get final score.

6. In Life mode you will play every round game.

7. In Challenge mode you must get enough balls (or less enough balls in use mode), so you can goto next round game.

8. In Perfection mode you must get every ball (or get none ball in use mode), so you can goto next round game.

How to play

Choose mode. There are Life Mode, Challenge Mode, Perfection Mode.

When the game begin, tap the screen. You create a big ball. The other balls collide the big ball. You get score.

Tap the screen for 1 seconds, there will show menu and pause the game.

In Life Mode and Perfection Mode, there will show the previous score on the top.

In Challenge Mode, there will show the at least||most collided balls on the top.

In Use Mode, you will expend your scores.

There will show your getted score, your used score and your final score.
