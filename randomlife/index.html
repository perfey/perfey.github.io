RandomLife rules

1. Click on the screen, get the big ball, when the normal ball collided the big ball, you will get score.

2. Every score multiplies the round number, then add they up, you will get total score.

3. If you get all balls in one round game, you will get more scores.

4. Obtained score minus used score, you will get final score.

5. In Life mode you will play every round game.

6. In Challenge mode you must get enough balls (or less enough balls in use mode), so you can goto next round game.

7. In Perfection mode you must get every ball (or get none ball in use mode), so you can goto next round game.

Life is random. You don't know who you will meet, which company you will worked in, what you will get in return.It is unknown, and this random makes wonderful fate.
